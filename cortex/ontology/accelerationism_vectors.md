# MATRIX ACC-001 TO ACC-020: Accelerationism Vectors

## Invariant Core
The contradictions of late capitalism cannot be resolved through regressive deceleration or neo-Luddite withdrawal. Emancipation is achieved by accelerating through the technological forces of production (automation, AGI, decentralized protocols) to split the capital-labor relation and trigger the transition to post-scarcity.

## AI Collapse Vector
Local and distributed execution of frontier models acts as the ultimate accelerator. By reducing the marginal cost of cognitive production to zero, the platform monopolies lose their capability to extract cognitive rent, forcing the social synthesis of the General Intellect.
