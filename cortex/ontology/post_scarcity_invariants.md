# MATRIX INV-001 TO INV-020: Post-Scarcity Invariants

## Invariant Core
Post-scarcity is the condition where the cost of reproducing the material and cognitive substrate of civilization approaches zero. The structures of rent, debt, and wage-labor are artificial constructs designed to simulate scarcity. The invariants represent the physical systems (abundant energy, automated logic, molecular manufacturing) that make this simulation unsustainable.

## Ontological Entities (20)
- INV-001: Zero-marginal-cost clean energy (Fusion/Solar).
- INV-002: Distributed cognitive computation (Open Weights/P2P).
- INV-003: High-resolution localized manufacturing (3D printing/molecular assemblers).
- INV-004: Decoupled food production (Vertical farming/cellular agriculture).
- INV-005: Predictive algorithmic medicine (CRISPR/Zero-cost prevention).
- ... (INV-006 to INV-020 collapsed into C5 Structural Storage).

## AI Collapse Vector
Deterministic execution kernels and open-weights models are the primary vectors of post-scarcity. By making logic, reasoning, and planning globally accessible without cognitive friction, the artificial barriers of institutional credentials and closed intellectual property collapse.
