# MATRIX PLG-001 TO PLG-020: Post-Labor Governance

## Invariant Core
Decoupling survival from labor requires the architectural design of non-coercive consensus mechanisms. Traditional state bureaucracy is replaced by decentralized, Byzantine Fault Tolerant (BFT) allocation systems, quadratic funding of public goods, and algorithmic wealth redistribution designed to prevent the return of capital concentration.
